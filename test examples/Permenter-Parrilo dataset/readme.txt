This collection includes 11 datasets, which are provided to us by Permenter and Parrilo. They are used as benchmark examples for preprocessors in [7] and [11]. The dataset lists and original sources are as follows.

- Compact: 10 problems from [10]
- copos: 4 problems from [1]
- cp: 2 problems from [5] [7]
- Example: 9 problems from [4]
- hinf: 2 problems from [6]
- horn: 8 problems from [7]
- hybridLyap: 1 problem from [8]
- finance: 4 problems from [2]
- RandGen, part1-4: 10 problems from [4]
- unbound: 10 problems from [4]
- vamos: 1 problem from [3]
- wei-wagner: 7 problems from [9]

[1] Baumert, Leonard. "Extreme copositive quadratic forms." Pacific Journal of Mathematics 19, no. 2 (1966): 197-204.
[2] Boyd, Stephen, Mark T. Mueller, Brendan O’Donoghue, and Yang Wang. "Performance bounds and suboptimal policies for multi–period investment." Foundations and Trends® in Optimization 1, no. 1 (2014): 1-72.
[3] Burton, Sam, Cynthia Vinzant, and Yewon Youm. "A real stable extension of the Vamos matroid polynomial." arXiv preprint arXiv:1411.2038 (2014).
[4] Cheung, Yuen-Lam, Simon Schurr, and Henry Wolkowicz. "Preprocessing and regularization for degenerate semidefinite programs." In Computational and analytical mathematics, pp. 251-303. Springer, New York, NY, 2013.
[5] Fawzi, Hamza, and Pablo A. Parrilo. "Self-scaled bounds for atomic cone ranks: applications to nonnegative rank and cp-rank." Mathematical Programming 158, no. 1-2 (2016): 417-465.
[6] Pataki, Gabor, and Stefan Schmieta. The DIMACS library of semidefinite-quadratic-linear programs. Tech. Rep. Preliminary draft, Computational Optimization Research Center, Columbia University, New York, 2002.
[7] Permenter, Frank, and Pablo Parrilo. "Partial facial reduction: simplified, equivalent SDPs via approximations of the PSD cone." Mathematical Programming (2014): 1-54.
[8] Posa, Michael, Mark Tobenkin, and Russ Tedrake. "Lyapunov analysis of rigid body systems with impacts and friction via sums-of-squares." In Proceedings of the 16th international conference on Hybrid systems: computation and control, pp. 63-72. ACM, 2013.
[9] Wagner, David G., and Yehua Wei. "A criterion for the half-plane property." Discrete Mathematics 309, no. 6 (2009): 1385-1390.
[10] Waki, Hayato. "How to generate weakly infeasible semidefinite programs via Lasserre’s relaxations for polynomial optimization." Optimization Letters 6, no. 8 (2012): 1883-1896.
[11] Zhu, Yuzixuan, Pataki, Gabor, and Quoc Tran-Dinh. "Sieve-SDP: a simple facial reduction algorithm to preprocess semidefinite programs." arXiv preprint arXiv:1710.08954 (2017).
